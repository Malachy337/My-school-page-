About Us

Sky Brain Secondary School, established in 2011, has a rich legacy of academic excellence and growth. Originally known as Dynamic Secondary School, the institution has undergone significant transformations over the years. In a pivotal move, the school was acquired by Hon. Samuel Anyanwu, a visionary leader committed to enhancing the educational experience for young minds. Under his guidance, the school was rebranded as Sky Brain Secondary School, with a renewed focus on academic excellence, innovation, and the holistic development of students. From its humble beginnings, Sky Brain Secondary School has continually evolved, striving to provide a nurturing environment that fosters the growth of well-rounded individuals who are prepared to excel in the world..

Our Vision

Sky Brain Secondary School aspires to be a premier institution, recognized for its unwavering commitment to academic excellence, integrity, and societal impact. Since receiving government and WAEC/NECO accreditation in 2018, we have consistently delivered exceptional results, with no record of failure in these critical examinations. Our vision is to continually produce well-rounded individuals who excel in academics and contribute meaningfully to society. Our proud alumni include individuals like Ossai Kingsley Chinedu, who is currently pursuing Medicine and Surgery at the University of Nigeria Teaching Hospital Enugu (UNTH), and Ossai Hillary Odinaka, studying Pharmacy at the University of Nigeria Nsukka. These successes are a testament to the foundation laid at Sky Brain Secondary School. Additionally, our school has nurtured talents like Chike Obi, a brilliant engineer who now leads groundbreaking projects in the tech industry; Maryam Bello, a passionate educator who is shaping the future of education in Nigeria; and Abiola Adebayo, a rising star in the entertainment industry, known for his contributions to the Nigerian film sector. At Sky Brain, we strive to shape the leaders of tomorrow—individuals who are not only academically proficient but also equipped with the moral values and resilience needed to thrive in an ever-evolving world.
